Aspect Ratio

I learned this the hard way.
When using OBS Studio, always use 'Fit To Screen' instead of 'Stretch To Screen' to preserve the original aspect ratio of your source.
I took a 4:3 source and stretched it to 16:9 and it looked awful.
If it's recorded in 4:3, keep it in 4:3.
If it's recorded in 16:9, keep it in 16:9.
You don't have to keep the black bars, but I'm keeping them since it reminds me of my childhood.
